Welcome to Valorant Agents!

Phoenix

Hailing from the U.K., Phoenix's star power shines through in his fighting style, igniting the battlefield with flash and flare. Whether he’s got backup or not, he’s rushing in to fight on his own terms.

Agent Abilities

Hot Hands

Throw a fireball that explodes after a delay or upon impact with the ground. The fire zone damages enemies and heals you.

Curveball

Cast a curving flare that bursts into brilliant light after a brief delay, temporarily blinding all looking at it. Left click curves it left, right click curves it right.

Blaze

Create a flame wall that blocks vision and damages anyone passing through it. You can bend the wall when casting by turning while holding the left mouse button.

Run It Back

Mark your current location. If you die during the ability’s duration, or when the ability's duration expires, you’ll be reborn at the marked location with full health.

Biography

Phoenix is a duelist offering strong offensive abilities and self-sustain. His kit encourages a bold playstyle that allows him to challenge opponents directly while offering some unique tools to outplay and outmaneuver his opponents.
