Agent A: The Mysterious

Agent A is a stealthy character with abilities to infiltrate and gather intel.

Passive: Shadow Walker

Agent A can move silently and leave no footprints.

Q: Smoke Screen

Deploys a smoke screen to obscure vision in an area.

E: Silent Step

Agent A can teleport a short distance silently.

Ultimate: Phantom Strike

Agent A becomes invisible and gains increased movement speed for a short duration.

Lore

Agent A comes from a background of covert operations, specializing in gathering intel without being detected.

Playstyle

Agent A excels at infiltrating enemy lines and gathering crucial information while avoiding direct confrontations.

Agent B: The Guardian

Agent B specializes in defensive strategies and protecting teammates.

Passive: Shield Wall

Agent B creates a shield that absorbs damage.

Q: Guardian Circle

Creates a protective barrier around an area that blocks enemy projectiles.

E: Healing Wave

Heals all teammates within a certain radius.

Ultimate: Fortify

Grants all teammates within range temporary invulnerability and increased armor.

Lore

Agent B has a history of serving in protective services, using his skills to safeguard his team in any situation.

Playstyle

Agent B is ideal for defensive play, protecting key areas and providing support to teammates under fire.

Agent C: The Disruptor

Agent C uses disruptive abilities to disorient and disable enemies.

Passive: Disorienting Aura

Enemies near Agent C have reduced accuracy.

Q: Flashbang

Throws a flashbang that blinds and disorients enemies in the area of effect.

E: Concussion Blast

Fires a blast that concusses and slows enemies hit.

Ultimate: Chaos Field

Deploys a field that silences enemy abilities and distorts their vision.

Lore

Agent C is a master of chaos, trained to disrupt enemy strategies and create openings for his team to strike.

Playstyle

Agent C thrives in causing confusion and disorder among enemies, making him a challenging opponent to face.
