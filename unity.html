Space Cowboy Platformer

Adventure through a 2D platformer level while fighting enemies until you reach the final boss, a cactus!

Overview

This project was made with a couple of my friends while I was at Sewanee. My main focus was on designing and implementing the final boss. He's a cactus that shoots out his spines in a radial pattern, and the fight increases in difficulty as you tear down his health bar! This was a blast to work on, and I'll forever remember the memories I made while working on this with my friends.

Use WASD to move the character, SPACE to jump, and LEFT CLICK to shoot. Enjoy!

Floppy Bird

A recreation of the wildly popular mobile game "Flappy Bird".

Overview

This was the first game that I ever fully finished in Unity, I did the all of the art and programming myself. Enjoy!

Try to survive as long as you can by timing your jumps to avoid the pipes! Press Space to jump.
