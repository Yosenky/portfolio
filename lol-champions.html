Welcome to LoL Champions

Aatrox, the Darkin Blade

Aatrox is a fearsome Darkin warrior with immense power and a terrifying presence on the battlefield.

Champion Abilities

Q: The Darkin Blade

Aatrox slams his greatsword down, dealing physical damage. This ability can be recast two additional times, each with a different area of effect.

W: Infernal Chains

Aatrox smashes the ground, dealing damage and chaining enemies hit. If they don't leave the impact area, they will be pulled back and damaged again.

E: Umbral Dash

Aatrox dashes, gaining bonus attack damage. This ability can also be cast during The Darkin Blade to increase repositioning potential.

R: World Ender

Aatrox unleashes his true demonic form, gaining bonus attack damage, movement speed, and a revival if he takes lethal damage.

Lore

Once honored defenders of Shurima against the Void, long ago the darkin fell to corruption and heroism became another word for meaningless slaughter. Aatrox and his brethren sought to conquer the known world, but instead they were defeated, their consciousnesses bound to arcane weaponry.
